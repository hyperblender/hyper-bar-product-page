Hyper Bar is a Blender add-on for animation renders. It adds a clean progress bar, and live render metrics, so you know how long your render will take and when it should finish.

Stop Guessing Render Time

Keep track of your render with clear, useful data: time left, estimated finish time, elapsed time, rendered and total frames, and average frame time.

Bar Location: the progress bar appears in the 3D Viewport sidebar and can optionally be shown in the render window header.

Rolling ETAHyper Bar estimates the time left based on recent frames. In the add-on settings, you can choose how many recent frames it uses, so the ETA can update faster or stay smoother.
